How to play

  • Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer Pac-Man through the maze.
  • Eat every pellet to advance; power pellets let you chomp ghosts for bonus points.
  • Queue your next move before an intersection to glide through turns like the arcade classic.
  • You have three lives. Colliding with a ghost without a power-up costs one life.
